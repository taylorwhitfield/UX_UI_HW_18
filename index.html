Mobile Design

P&F Pet Provisions Website Redesign

The Problem: After reviewing the P&F Pet Provisions website, we found a number of flow and design problems. Although the site contained all of the products a pet owner needs, it was difficult to navigate and the design was distracting when browsing the site.
The Solution: Redesign the navigation and pet food sections of the P&F Pet Provisions website so it is easier to navigate and users can quickly access information on products and purchase items for their pets.

Mobile App Design

restore

Recycle? Compost? Landfill? We created an app, Restore, to understand the motivating factors behind why people do or don’t practice eco-friendly habits and encourage and empower users to develop environmentally conscious habits by providing them with the tools to do so.

Desktop and Mobile Design

Secret Service Website Redesign

The goal of this project was to redesign the mobile and desktop Secret Service website to create a clearer design pattern and combine information in a more cohesive way for the user. I worked to fix the site flow and combine different pages within the site into one in order to create a more cohesive and easy to navigate site.

Mobile App Design

Troop

Troop. is a travel app I created to better understand the tools travelers use to schedule and coordinate group trips and their pain points throughout the process. The goal was to offer travelers an easy way to customize and create itineraries when traveling with larger groups.
